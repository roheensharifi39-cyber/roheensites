# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Students, creators, local businesses, restaurants, and growing brands that need a credible, mobile-friendly web presence without an agency-sized process or budget. Visitors are usually comparing examples, pricing, and contact options quickly from a phone.

## Product Purpose

Sites by Roheen presents Roheen Sharifi's website-building service, proves capability through live examples, explains practical pricing and scope, and turns interested visitors into direct inquiries or payments.

## Brand Personality

Direct, capable, approachable. The experience should feel professional and technically confident while keeping pricing and communication practical.

## Anti-references

Avoid agency theater, generic template-heavy landing pages, excessive glass effects, motion-heavy portfolios, decorative animation that competes with the work, and interactions that make the service feel expensive or difficult to use.

## Design Principles

1. Show real proof before making claims.
2. Make the mobile path fast, legible, and easy to act on.
3. Use motion to establish hierarchy, never as background noise.
4. Keep pricing and next steps plain and specific.
5. Preserve a direct personal connection to Roheen rather than presenting an anonymous agency.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA fundamentals, maintain visible keyboard focus, preserve readable contrast, use semantic content and descriptive image text, and fully respect reduced-motion preferences. All critical content and actions must remain usable without animation.
